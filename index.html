About Our Research Initiative

HHR Quantitative Research is an independent educational research initiative focused on advancing the understanding of foreign exchange markets through rigorous quantitative analysis and backtesting methodologies.

Our mission is to contribute to the academic and practitioner communities by conducting transparent, reproducible research that enhances market understanding and promotes best practices in quantitative finance.

Our Core Principles

  • Educational Purpose: All research is conducted for learning and academic advancement
  • Non-Commercial: No profit motive or commercial application of findings
  • Transparency: Commitment to open methodology and reproducible results
  • Ethical Data Use: Strict compliance with data provider terms and licensing

Data Ethics & Compliance

Responsible Data Stewardship

We are committed to the highest standards of data ethics and compliance in our research activities.

Data Sources

We use historical FX price data from reputable providers such as Dukascopy, accessed in full compliance with their terms of service and licensing requirements.

Data Usage

  • Stored locally for research purposes only
  • Restricted access limited to research personnel
  • No redistribution or commercial resale
  • Compliance with all rate limits and usage policies

Publication Standards

Any published results contain only aggregated statistics and insights. Raw data is never redistributed. All findings are presented for educational and research purposes only.

Research Outputs & Publications

Our research findings are shared with the academic and practitioner communities to advance collective knowledge.
